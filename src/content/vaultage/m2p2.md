## overall thoughts
yeah, its a good album. imo its nothing special because it sounds like most other wonky minimal riddim ive heard before but the sound design is good. tracks seem super repetitive–they all, in some way, shape or form, reuse and sample the super wet riddim wub, which makes the songs feel bland and less memorable when played one after the other. it feels like more of a compilation of random songs rather than a journey (the intro and outro dont transition in and out of songs respectively.)
can confirm its music 2 paint 2. unironically something i would listen to while doing some kind of art lol.
cover art is super creative, i love it.

overall super good sound design and songs nontheless despite everything feeling almost the same. my only problem with it is that it feels less like an album
and more a compilation of songs thrown together randomly with and intro an outro to make it seem like one.
